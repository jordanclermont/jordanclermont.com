01

Student Handbook
Visual Identity

Brand Print Publication
The brief

SAITSA's student handbook is the single most-distributed print piece the organization produces — 70+ pages, handed to every incoming student. It needed a visual identity that felt intentional, cohesive, and worth keeping rather than recycling.

The approach

Built a design system grounded in consistent typography, structured layouts, and a colour palette that carried across both print and digital touchpoints. The same visual language later informed the email bulletin rebuild and event materials.
