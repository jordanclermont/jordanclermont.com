01

Student Handbook
Visual Identity

Brand Print Publication
The brief

SAITSA's student handbook is the single most-distributed print piece the organization produces — 70+ pages, handed to every incoming student. It had to look like something worth keeping — not recycling by week one.

The approach

Cover-to-cover design across multiple editions — the 2019–2020 Survival Guide, the 2020/21 handbook, and onward. Each edition built a system of consistent typography, structured layouts, and a colour palette that carried across print and digital. The same visual language later informed the email bulletin rebuild and event materials.

02

Campus Campaigns
& Event Posters

Print Social Campaigns
The work

Years of event and campaign creative for SAITSA — drag bingo at the Gateway (the campus bar, now demolished but never forgotten), federal election engagement, Calgary Flames student nights, clubs week, and campus safety programs. Every piece had to fit the Saitsa* brand and still stand out on a crowded campus corkboard.

The constraint

Each campaign shipped in multiple formats at once: tabloid posters for the walls, squares for Instagram, wide crops for campus TV screens, rack cards for the resource centre. One idea, five canvases, usually one week.

03

Menus, Icons
& Side Quests

Menus Illustration Zine
Food & beverage

Menu systems for SAITSA's food and beverage venues — the Odyssey, the campus coffee shop every SAIT student of the last decade knows by name, plus the Station and campus catering — and the digital signage that promoted them. Seasonal feature boards, TV-screen ads, and a custom icon set so the food programs had their own visual shorthand.

And for fun

King Creatus — a self-published zine that teaches the principles and elements of graphic design through a hand-drawn wizard king of creativity. Because the best way to prove you understand design fundamentals is to explain them with a mustachioed pencil monarch.
